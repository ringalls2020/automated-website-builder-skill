# Outreach Interview

Use this reference before drafting messages. Interview the user after reading the lead sheet, so questions can be specific.

## Ask These Questions When Missing

1. Sender identity: name, company, role, and the reply address or preferred callback channel.
2. Offer: what Tocayo/demo-site service is being pitched, pricing if any, and what happens after the prospect replies.
3. Target segment: which lead types to prioritize and which to avoid.
4. Channel: email, SMS, LinkedIn, Instagram DM, Facebook DM, or phone script.
5. Tone: direct, friendly, consultative, premium, casual, bilingual, or another style.
6. CTA: book a call, reply with interest, view demo, approve changes, or claim the site.
7. Constraints: max message length, compliance language, forbidden claims, required links, geography, and sending volume.

Ask compactly. If the user asks to proceed without a full interview, use the defaults below and state the assumptions.

## Sensible Defaults

- Sender identity: infer from available project/user context only when obvious; otherwise ask.
- Channel: email if an email exists, otherwise DM or SMS only if the user confirms it is appropriate.
- Tone: concise, friendly, specific, and low-pressure.
- CTA: "Want me to send over the live preview?"
- Message length: under 120 words for email, under 50 words for DMs.
- Follow-up delay: 3 to 5 business days.

## Message Rules

- Lead with a concrete observation from public evidence.
- Mention that a quick preview/demo was built, then link the live Tocayo site and attach or reference the MP4.
- Avoid "I noticed your website is bad" framing. Use neutral phrases like "I mocked up a cleaner version" or "I put together a quick preview."
- Do not invent customer names, revenue numbers, reviews, rankings, or prior conversations.
- Do not imply the prospect requested the site.
- For email, include a simple opt-out line when sending at scale.

## Output Variants

For each lead, write:

- A primary email or DM.
- A shorter alternate version.
- One follow-up message.
- Optional phone opener if the user asked for calls.

Keep the copy editable and specific. Do not overexplain Tocayo in the message; the live site and MP4 should carry the proof.
